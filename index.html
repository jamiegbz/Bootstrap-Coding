Stitch

Stitch was created by Dr. Jumba, he is a mad scientist from space. Dr. Jumba created stitch, so he could cause chaos and distruction acroos the glaxy. Stitch was known as Experiment 626. Before, creating experiment 626 Dr.Jumba created 625 others, but experiment 626 was designed to be imdestructible. The Intergalactic police found out about Dr. Jumba and experiment 626, and arrested them and took them to planet Turo to stand trial. Dr. Jumba and experiment 626 were found guilty, and 626 was going to be exiled on a desert asteroid. 626 was able to escape and stole a federal police cruiser. 626 activated the cruiser's hyperdrive, but it crash landed on Earth on the island of Kauai.

After, landing on Kauai he was ran over by trucks. Knocking him out, and was taken to the local dog shelter. When 626 woke up, he tried to escape but failed. Moments later Lilo walked in the pet shelter looking for a pet dog. 626 saw his opportunity, and he altered his appearance to look like a dog, so Lilo could adopt him. Lilo saw 626 and adopted him. Lilo saw 626, and decied to name him Stitch.
