Stitch's Family

Lilo

Lilo is a Hawaiian girl who lives with her older sister Nani. Lilo went to a pet shelter to look for a dog. Lilo saw 626 and decied to adopt him. When they arrived at their house Lilo named 626 Stitch, and this was the begining of there forever fiendship.

Nani

Nani is Lilo's older sister, and her legal guardian, due to their parent's death. Nani cares for Lilo, but like older sisters they have their arguements. Nani tries to keep their household in check even with all the chaos.

Pleakley

Pleakley is from the planet Plorgonar. He use to work for the Galatic Federation, and is an "expert" on planet Earth. Pleakley met Dr. Jumba when they are partnered up to capture Stitch "Experiment-626". Pleakley and Dr. Jumba later become best friends and roommates when they decied to stay on Earth.

Dr. Jumba

Dr Jumba is from planet Quelte Quan and the creator of Experiment-626. When Dr. Jumba created his experiments he wishes that they would succeed and others would recognize him as an evil genius. When Dr. Jumba is sent to go to Earth to capture Sitch with Pleakley, it is seen that Dr. Jumba in reality is a good frined and intelligent.
